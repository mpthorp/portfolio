a bit about me

A User Experience Designer with over 13 years of experience in design and font­end development.

I began creating websites at the age of 17 in my hometown Auckland, New Zealand. At the age of 25 I head to the EU for my OE and have since been floating around the world for 6 years working for companies in London, Amsterdam and Lisbon.

It was time for me to head closer to home and being the world's most liveable city, Melbourne seemed a great place to perch.

So here I am and I've brought a full spectrum of experience with me. A master in Photoshop and Illustrator, I'm also advanced in HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript with plenty of experience in mobile app design, flash, print media and HTML emails.

I design and build responsive websites using the mobile first and modular methodologies. I enjoy seeing the entire process through including concept, wire­framing, design, development and user testing.

In my pass time, I enjoy making music, snowboarding, wakeboarding, rugby, cricket, tennis and am always happy to join ya for that after work drink ;D
