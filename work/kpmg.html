KPMG Marketplace is a pilot app we at etaskr developed with KPMG.

Similar to the Etaskr app, KPMG Marketplace allows KPMG clients to post work so that employees with some spare time can pick up extra paid work.

KPMG Marketplace has proven to increase productivity with $30,000 worth of extra business generated within the first 2 weeks.
