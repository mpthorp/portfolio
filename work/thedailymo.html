the daily mo

The Daily Mo was a project I took on in support of Movember which is a charity that raises funds for prostate cancer research.

Users could post the progress of their moustache to their profile to show off and keep record of it's growth.

Those without the ability to grow, could use the "Mo Selecta" to position a moustache of their choice on their photo and save it to The Daily Mo Poser's wall.
